Who We Are

Pure
Energy.
No Filler.


RELENTLESS is the Bay Area's most dangerous dance band. Every song has been hand-picked to get your feet moving and your arms in the air. There is no slow moment. There is no breather. Just relentless energy from the first note to the last.

Whether you're a September and Don't Stop Believin' devotee, or you need Uptown Funk and Blinding Lights to get you moving — we've got you. Guns N' Roses fans? Killers heads? Dua Lipa stans? We will destroy you all equally.

Every song is a weapon. Every performance is an event. Every night ends the same way: wanting more.
